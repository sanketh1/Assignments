Chicken

The chicken is a domesticated subspecies of the red junglefowl, with attributes of wild species such as grey and ceylon junglefowl that are originally from Southeastern Asia.

Beef

Beef is the culinary name for meat from cattle. In prehistoric times, humans hunted aurochs and later domesticated them.

Sushi

Sushi is a Japanese dish of prepared vinegared rice, usually with some sugar and salt, accompanied by a variety of ingredients, such as seafood, often raw, and vegetables.
